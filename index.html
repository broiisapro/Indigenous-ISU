Bad Cree by Jessica Johns, a website made by @broiisapro on github (that is me)

This website serves as a comprehensive digital portfolio for our English class project, delving into the intricacies of Bad Cree by Jessica Johns. Throughout these pages, you will find a multi-faceted exploration of the novel, combining literary analysis, in-depth research, and creative expression.

My goal is to offer a unique perspective on the themes, characters, and historical context that defines Bad Cree. Navigate through each section, from our original podcast discussion to detailed research findings and a concluding synthesis of my discoveries to broaden your view.

Thank you for reading this far I hope this resource enhances your understanding and appreciation of this work of literature.
